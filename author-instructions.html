Instructions for authors

We have freshly updated (July 7, 2017) the paper templates, which must be used for the camera ready submissions.

Get them below.

  • Word template
  • LaTeX template

It is important that authors observe the format requirement; please do not alter the templates and do not use LaTeX commands to alter the spacings around headers and margins.

In the final camera ready copy, authors are allowed one additional page from their submission drafts to address concerns raised by the reviewers. That is, a long paper can have up to nine (9) pages of content with unlimited pages for references, and a short paper can have up to five (5) pages of content with unlimited pages for references.

The camera ready files should be submitted via Softconf.

The deadline is July 21, 2017
