Guide to Copenhagen

Where to eat, drink, and have fun

The local organisers have been busy preparing a guide highlighting their favourite restaurants, bars, and museums, as wells as leafy parks around the city to just hang out and relax.

The guide is available as pdf. Follow its advice, and you're practically guaranteed to have a good time.

Visit the Techfestival (free access)

EMNLP overlaps with Copenhagen’s Techfestival, which also takes place in the Meatpacking District (Kødbyen), September 5-10. The Techfestival program includes an open air main stage, exhibitions, music, meet-ups, and parties. Its themes include privacy, cybersecurity, blockchain, machine ethics, AI, and more.

See the full program at www.Techfestival.co

The EMNLP conference bag includes a wristband that provides you with free access. You can also claim your wristband online and join the festival before EMNLP begins: Use promo code “TechfestivalXemnlp" to get your wristband via eventbrite.
