What do the categories mean? Full-Pack entitles the attendee to attend the main conference plus all tutorials and workshops desired. Main + 1 Day entitles the attendee to attend the main conference plus one day of tutorials and workshops. 1-Day entitles the attendee to attend only one day of tutorials and workshops (no main conference). 2-Day entitles the attendee to attend both workshops and tutorials days but not the main conference.
