Booking trouble at DGI byen

Unfortunately, the hotel DGI byen only has a Danish website, which makes it challenging to book unless you speak the language. We've put together a small document with advice on how to deal with this.

Room sharing

Rooms in Copenhagen are getting sparse. If you are looking for roommates to save money on lodging, sign up at conferenceshare.co to find attendees with whom you can share hotel room, Airbnb, taxi, or car rental.
